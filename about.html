Who We are
Leaders in Car Hire

Impala Car Rental is a market driven entity and a formidable partner in the hospitality & tourism industry specializing in car rental services and packages. For over a decade we have been offering a full range of car rental services including but not limited to self-drive, chauffer drive as well as shuttle services. Impala prides itself by being manned by experienced and dedicated front and back office as well support staff whose commitment to the delivery of superior car rental service is unparalleled. It is our strategy to continue revamping and edifying our fleet with the latest and durable vehicles to meet and exceed the growing customer needs of the market we serve.

Impala once considered a lightweight has risen like the phoenix, submerging all completion like the legendary Bermuda triangle. Over the past 10 years our trophy cabinet has bustled with awards including the super brand best car rental 2017, businessman of the year, best car rental customer choice award 2017, most trusted brand CICRM 2017 to mention only a few. Scooping the trophies wouldn't have been made possible had it not been for the loyal clients that have been our backbone since time immemorial; to date we strive to reward such loyalty as it is the mainstay of our business to remain a going concern. Regardless of the times our services have always been outstanding and left customers with no qualms about using our services. We pride ourselves in service excellence, professionalism, reliability and flexibility.
